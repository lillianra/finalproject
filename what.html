             What are Mayan Scripts?
                The Mayan hieroglyphic writing system was a sophisticated combination of pictographs and glyphs.
                

                           
                

            What do Mayan texts deal with?
                A large amount of the surviving ancient Maya texts, especially on stone monuments, deal with the lives of kings and queens,, when they were born, who they married, when they died, 
                and who followed them. Many texts contain a large amount of calendrical information, starting with a very detailed description of the date as well as details of astronomical events, 
                followed by what happened at this point in time, followed by who was doing it or to who it was being done to.
